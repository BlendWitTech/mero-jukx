# Mero Jugx — User Guide

**The Operating System for Nepali Business**

Mero Jugx is an all-in-one business platform built specifically for Nepali companies. Instead of juggling multiple tools, everything your business needs — accounting, payroll, inventory, CRM, HR, project management, and more — lives in one place, all in one subscription.

---

## What is Mero Jugx?

Mero Jugx is a **multi-tenant SaaS ERP** (Enterprise Resource Planning) platform. Each business gets its own isolated workspace. You can invite your team, control what each person can see and do, and subscribe to only the apps you need.

Think of it as an **app store for your business** — you start with the core platform and add modules as your needs grow.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Organization
Go to the sign-up page and register your business. You'll provide:
- Your business name
- Your business email
- Your name and password

You'll become the **Organization Owner** with full access to everything.

### 2. Invite Your Team
From **Settings → Members**, invite team members by email. Assign each person a role:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything |
| **Admin** | Manage users, apps, settings — everything except billing upgrades |
| **Manager** | Team management, reporting, task assignment |
| **Employee** | Day-to-day work within their assigned apps |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access |

You can also create **custom roles** with exactly the permissions you need.

### 3. Subscribe to Apps
Go to the **App Marketplace** and subscribe to the modules your business needs. Each app can be assigned to specific team members.

## Payments & Billing

Mero Jugx supports Nepal's most popular payment gateways:
- **eSewa** — Nepal's leading digital wallet
- **Khalti** — Digital payment platform
- **ConnectIPS** — Bank-to-bank transfers (NCHL)
- **IME Pay** — Mobile money service

All amounts are in **NPR (Nepalese Rupees)** with full VAT support.

## Nepal-Specific Features

Mero Jugx is built from the ground up for Nepal:

- **Bikram Sambat (BS) calendar** — All dates can be entered and viewed in BS or AD
- **VAT at 13%** — Automatic VAT calculation and invoice generation
- **TDS** — Tax deducted at source with proper rate handling
- **PAN/VAT number validation** — Automatic format validation for Nepali PAN and VAT numbers
- **PF compliance** — Provident fund at 10% employee + 10% employer
- **NPR currency** — All financials in Nepalese Rupees
- **Annex-7 export** — For IRD (Inland Revenue Department) VAT filing

## Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

- **JWT authentication** — Secure login with short-lived access tokens
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA/MFA)** — Optional TOTP-based 2FA via Google Authenticator or similar apps
- **Role-based access control (RBAC)** — Granular permission control down to individual actions
- **Organization isolation** — Your data is completely isolated from other businesses on the platform
- **Audit logs** — Every action is logged for compliance and security review
- **Session management** — Multiple device support with the ability to revoke sessions

## Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Each organization's data is fully isolated. We use bank-grade encryption and follow security best practices.

The platform currently operates in English with Nepal-specific data formats (BS calendar, NPR, PAN/VAT). Nepali language support is planned.
